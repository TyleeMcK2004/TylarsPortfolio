A little bit about me...

Hi! I'm Tylar, a 20-year-old website developer-in-training from the beautiful fields of Aberdeenshire, Scotland.

I grew up surrounded by rolling hills, muddy boots, and good-hearted people. Now I’m living in Glasgow, studying Graphic Media, but my love for the countryside never left.

Photography has been my way of capturing memories, feelings, and the occasional mischief of farm life. This portfolio is my personal journey, and I'm thrilled to share it with you.
